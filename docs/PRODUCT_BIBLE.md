# Fabled Compass Product Bible

## Mission

Fabled Compass helps people discover, develop, demonstrate, and advance professionally through evidence-based growth.

## Product Mantra

Every feature should help someone become more employable, more confident, or more connected.

## North Star Question

Does this help someone discover, develop, demonstrate, or advance professionally?

If the answer is no, the feature should wait or be redesigned.

## Core Principles

1. Professional first.
2. Evidence over claims.
3. Growth over gamification.
4. Calm interfaces create confident users.
5. Every interaction should have purpose.
6. Consistency builds trust.
7. Community supports growth.
8. Technology should disappear behind the experience.
9. Respect the user's time.
10. The compass always points forward.

## Scoring Principles

- Never show a score without explaining it.
- Every score must have reasons.
- Every score must lead to an action.
- Compass is a coach, not a judge.
- Scores should guide improvement, not label worth.

## The Four-Part Journey

### Discover

Self-reflection and assessment.

Examples:

- Personality assessments
- Work preferences
- Communication style
- Conflict style
- Career interests

### Develop

Learning, mentorship, practice, and support.

Examples:

- Mentors
- Groups
- Learning paths
- Practice recommendations
- AI coaching

### Demonstrate

Workplace challenges and scenario evidence.

Examples:

- Customer scenarios
- Interview simulations
- Leadership challenges
- AI roleplay
- Scenario reports

### Advance

Career movement and opportunity.

Examples:

- Jobs
- Applications
- Interviews
- Employer conversations
- Portfolio exports

## Primary Systems

### Navigation

The app shell, journey-aware navigation, theme engine, sidebar, top bar, and workspace layout.

### Career

Dashboard, Portfolio, Jobs, Applications, Employers, Portfolio Strength, Career Readiness, and Job Match.

### Community

Friends, groups, guilds, mentors, events, networking, and professional communities.

### Adventure

Assessments, challenges, AI roleplay, NPCs, competencies, scenario reports, and the Professional Compass.

### Communication Hub

Compass AI, direct messages, recruiter chat, employer chat, group chat, notifications, and out-of-character scenario support.

### Navigator Center

Account, privacy, accessibility, downloads, themes, notifications, and user preferences.

## AI Philosophy: Compass

Compass is not a hidden decision maker.

Compass is:

- a coach
- a guide
- an explainer
- a teacher
- a writing assistant
- a planning partner

Compass should help users understand what is happening, why it matters, and what they can do next.

## Communication Philosophy

The Communication Hub is not a separate app. It is a second layer of the workspace.

The main page is where the user works.

The Communication Hub is where conversations happen.

It supports:

- Compass AI
- direct messages
- friends
- groups
- recruiters
- employers
- out-of-character roleplay support
- notifications
- shared platform objects

## Portfolio Philosophy

The Portfolio is the center of the platform.

It contains:

- professional summary
- contact information
- experience
- education
- certifications
- projects
- skills
- resume library
- proven competencies
- scenario evidence
- downloads

The user must be able to export what they build.

## Professional Compass

The Professional Compass visualizes demonstrated competencies.

It is not based on self-ratings alone.

Each direction grows through evidence from scenarios, assessments, projects, and validated experiences.

## What Fabled Compass Is Not

Fabled Compass is not:

- a LinkedIn clone
- an Indeed clone
- a Discord clone
- a game-first roleplay platform
- a black-box hiring score
- a social feed

Fabled Compass is a career operating system built around evidence, growth, and direction.
