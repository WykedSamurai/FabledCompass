# Product Vision

Fabled Compass combines job discovery with interactive workplace roleplay.

## Mission

Help people practice, demonstrate, and communicate workplace skills while finding meaningful opportunities.

## Vision

Build a trusted career platform where demonstrated behavior complements resumes, education, and work history.

## Product Pillars

### Careers
A job board for candidates and employers.

### Adventures
Workplace scenarios that provide structured feedback and evidence-based badges.

### Community
A later professional and social roleplay area for mentoring, groups, events, and cooperative practice.

## Primary Users

- Job seekers
- Employers
- Platform administrators
- Scenario authors

## Product Principles

1. Professional usefulness comes before game mechanics.
2. Badges must represent documented criteria, not participation alone.
3. Feedback must be tied to observable decisions.
4. Users must know what employers can view.
5. Scoring must be understandable and reviewable.
6. The first release must remain narrow enough to test.
7. Social features must not undermine safety or professional credibility.

## Brand Direction

The product should feel professional, welcoming, and quietly adventurous. Use deep navy, forest green, bronze or gold accents, compass motifs, and restrained fantasy elements.

## Long-Term Direction

Possible later features include employer-created scenarios, learning paths, AI coaching, mentoring, cooperative scenarios, career communities, verified credentials, and premium employer tools.
