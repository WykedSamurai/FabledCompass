# User Flows

## Job Seeker: First Visit

1. Open the landing page.
2. Select **Begin Your Journey**.
3. Create an account.
4. Choose **Job Seeker**.
5. Complete a basic profile.
6. Arrive at the personal dashboard.
7. Choose to browse jobs or begin an adventure.

## Job Seeker: Complete a Scenario

1. Open the scenario library.
2. Select **The Difficult Customer**.
3. Review the scenario description, skills assessed, estimated length, and possible badge.
4. Begin the scenario.
5. Read each scene and choose a response.
6. Complete the final decision.
7. View the result, skill feedback, and score explanation.
8. Earn the Customer Service Foundations badge when all requirements are met.
9. View the badge on the profile.

## Job Seeker: Browse Jobs

1. Open the job board.
2. Search or filter listings.
3. Open a job detail page.
4. Review company, responsibilities, requirements, location, and application method.
5. Save the job or begin the application process.

## Employer: First Visit

1. Create an account.
2. Choose **Employer**.
3. Create a company profile.
4. Open the employer dashboard.
5. Create the first job listing.
6. Publish the listing.

## Employer: Review a Candidate

1. Open the employer dashboard.
2. Select a job listing.
3. Open an applicant profile.
4. Review experience and profile information.
5. Review earned badges.
6. Open a badge detail to see its criteria and source scenario.

## Administrator: Content Management

1. Log in with an administrator role.
2. Review job listings requiring moderation.
3. Publish, reject, close, or flag listings.
4. Review scenario and badge definitions.
5. Inspect reported content or account concerns.

## Navigation Model

Primary navigation:

- Jobs
- Adventures
- Profile
- Dashboard

Employer navigation:

- Jobs
- Post a Job
- Applicants
- Company Profile

Community navigation is excluded from the MVP.
