# Atlas Codex
## Volume I — The Vision

> Every career is an adventure. Every professional has a story.

Welcome, Traveler.

Atlas exists to help people build a Professional Character through growth, evidence, and meaningful experience. It is inspired by the sense of progression found in tabletop role-playing games, while remaining an original world dedicated to professional development.

## Our Purpose
- Character over titles.
- Evidence over claims.
- Growth over perfection.
- Mentorship over competition.
- Legacy over résumé.

## The Seven Principles
1. Growth is Earned.
2. Character Outlasts Titles.
3. Evidence Builds Trust.
4. Every Journey Matters.
5. Learning Never Ends.
6. Mentorship Multiplies Growth.
7. Leave a Legacy.

## The Professional Character
Every traveler develops:
- Background
- Archetype
- Professions
- Attributes
- Competencies
- Evidence
- Career Experience
- Milestones
- Campaign Journal
- Legacy

## Atlas Motto
**Build a Professional. Leave a Legacy.**
